About KOSL

Making chips
like making PCBs

We're two guys in Toronto trying to make semiconductor fabrication something you can do at home. Open source, safe chemicals, cheap hardware.

The photoresist is egg whites. The photosensitizer is vitamin B2. The etchant is drain cleaner. The developer is water. The whole setup is a $525 CNC machine with a laser bolted on.
