About KOSL

KOSL is an applied sciences lab based in Toronto, developing open source processes and equipment for home semiconductor fabrication. Our goal is to make chip-making as accessible as making PCBs.

Every process we develop follows three principles: no toxic chemicals, no cleanroom required, and no million-dollar equipment. All research, designs, and experiment results are published under permissive licenses.

Open Source All designs, processes, and research published under permissive licenses.
Accessible No specialty chemicals. Everything from Amazon and the grocery store.
Safe No HF. No organic solvents. Water-based development. Grocery store etchants.
